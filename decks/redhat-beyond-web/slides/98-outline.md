## Content Types

### Sectioning Content

Defines the scope of `<header>` and `<footer>` elements 

- `<article>`
- `<aside>`
- `<nav>`
- `<section>`

### Heading Content

- `<h1>`, `<h2>`, `<h3>`, `<h4>`, `<h5>`, `<h6>`

- Only one `<h1>` per page
- Headings must not skip ahead, so no `<h1>` to `<h3>`
- But you may skip back, to `<h6>` to `<h2>`

### Phrasing Content

The text of the document

- `a`, `abbr`
